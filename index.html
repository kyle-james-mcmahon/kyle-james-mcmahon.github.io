About Me

I am a globally engaged research scientist specializing in CRISPR-based diagnostics, high-throughput assay development, and outbreak response. With international field deployments and deep expertise in lab automation, I translate molecular insights into actionable public health solutions.

Research Highlights

  • CARMEN Diagnostic Platform: Led development and deployment for Lassa, Ebola, Dengue detection in West Africa. Co-authored peer-reviewed publications.
  • Outbreak Assays: Designed RT-qPCR/dPCR pipelines for H5N1 milk surveillance (collaborated with MDAR & MDPH) and rapid-response Oropouche assay under simulated outbreak conditions.
  • Lab Automation: Implemented workflow improvements across Agilent Bravo, Hamilton STARlet, Dynamic Devices Lynx, and QuantStudio 7 for enhanced throughput.
